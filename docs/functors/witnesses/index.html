A Faithful but not Full Functor

Name: A Faithful but not Full Functor

Definition:

The inclusion functor $i: \mathbf{InjSet} \to \mathbf{Set}$ from the category of sets with injective maps to the category of sets is faithful but not full, since not every function between sets is injective.
